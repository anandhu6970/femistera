Championing
Equality For Women
In Nigerian Society.
The feminist coalition is a group of young Nigerian feminists
formed in July 2020 with a mission to champion equality for
women in Nigerian society with a core focus on education,
financial freedom and representation in public office.
We have a vision for a Nigeria where equality for all people is a
reality in our laws and everyday lives.
In July 2020, Damilola Odufuwa and Odunayo Eweniyi decided to come together to create a coalition of feminist women with a vision of a Nigeria where equality for all people is a reality in our laws and everyday lives.



We approached 12 other exceptional women who were equally passionate about changing our country and the role of women.



Together with these women we became the Feminist Coalition. The Feminist Coalition aims to further the rights of Nigerian women by focusing on three (3) pillars: women’s rights & safety, financial equality for women; and political & legislative power for women.
